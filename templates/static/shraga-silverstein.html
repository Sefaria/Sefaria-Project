Rabbi Shraga Silverstein זצ״ל

Rabbi Shraga Silverstein was a Rabbi, author, and translator. He translated a number of books, including seventeen that were not published by 2014, and wrote three books himself. He lived in Jerusalem until his death in 2014.

Rabbi Shraga Silverstein graduated from Brooklyn College summa cum laude, with honours in English. He was also an alumnus of Mesivta Rabbi Chaim Berlin. He taught in universities in the United States and Israel and served as Principal of various yeshivas.

Since his untimely passing, Sefaria has been working with his family to obtain the rights to his unpublished works in order to make them widely available. Everything that is available on Sefaria is done so under the CC-BY license. We hope that this library will become a living memorial and make the works of Rabbi Silverstein as accesible as possible. What you see right now is just the beginning - we're going to keep adding his works to Sefaria and will keep this page updated as we do.
