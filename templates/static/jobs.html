Education Assistant (Temporary)

Position Overview

Sefaria is seeking a temporary Education Assistant in Israel. This six-month position will support Sefaria's Israeli educational initiatives.

  • Support Sefaria's social media presence (in Hebrew)
  • Gather and create materials for teachers and educators
  • Logistical support for Sefaria's education team in Israel
  • Administrative support for Sefaria's partnerships with other educational organizations

Requirements

  • A high level of Jewish textual fluency
  • Extensive experience using Sefaria
  • Ability to read, write, and speak fluently in both Hebrew and English
  • Proficiency with social media
  • Proactive and self-driven worker
  • Detail oriented and organized

This position will run through December 2019, and will be approximately 10 hours of work per week. This role also has the potential to grow into something more expansive in the future. Hourly rate commensurate with experience.

To Apply

Please send a resume and brief cover letter to jobs@sefaria.org with the subject line “Education Assistant”.

DevOps/Backend Engineer

Position Overview

Sefaria is looking for an entrepreneurial and outgoing professional to join its core engineering team. The position will be involved in the ongoing development of Sefaria’s software and will be responsible for planning, implementing and maintaining systems and development operations.

Sefaria has a sizable code base, mainly in Python and js/React. We have a mobile app, written in React Native. Our environment includes Linux, Nginx, Varnish, Apache, Node, and MongoDB on Google Cloud / Docker / Kubernetes. We also use Google Analytics and Tag Manager for analytics, and Pytest, Travis-CI, SauceLabs and Selenium for testing.

Responsibilities include

  • Configuring and maintaining our production environment
  • Improving and maintaining our testing/QA environments and toolsets
  • Management of our analytics infrastructure
  • Designing and managing our deployment process
  • Software development of new features and improvements to our backend systems

    • Requirements

  • 5+ Years experience in an active production environment
  • Proven experience with mid-sized production deployments
  • Comfort working in a dynamic, start-up environment
  • Strong communication, judgment, and decision-making skills
  • Proactive and self-driven worker

    • Location & Compensation

    Reports to the Chief Data Officer who is based in New York

    Salary range is $80-100k plus benefits (based on location)

    Sefaria has a presence in New York, Denver, Chicago, St. Louis, Boston, the Bay Area, and Jerusalem. US and Israel-based candidates will be considered.

    To Apply

    Email jobs@sefaria.org with the subject line “DevOps/Backend Engineer” and attach a brief cover letter and resume (PDFs preferred). All materials should be in English.

    About Sefaria: Sefaria is a non-profit organization dedicated to building the future of Jewish learning in an open and participatory way.
