Jobs at Sefaria משרות פנויות בספריא



Part-time Writer / Editor

About Sefaria

Sefaria is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating the future of Torah in an open and participatory way. We are assembling a free, living library of Jewish texts and their interconnections, in Hebrew and in translation.

Position Overview

Sefaria is seeking a freelance writer and editor to support our work in making Jewish texts accessible online.

Responsibilities

  • Explain complex Jewish texts and topics in simple language and easily accessible concepts, while thinking critically about how they are being presented.
  • Edit and improve existing language on the Sefaria website, apps, and google ads.
  • Ensure that the copy conforms to Sefaria's guidelines for product voice, and develop those guidelines further based on feedback from the team.
  • Adapt copy for length limitations and site context.
  • Identify and fix technical errors in the copy, including inaccuracies, grammar, and typos.

Requirements

  • Substantial knowledge about the Jewish textual cannon and Jewish culture
  • Experience writing copy for a publication or similar service, preferably for websites or other digital platforms
  • Experience writing for broad, general audiences
  • 20 or more hours per week of availability
  • Fluent in English
  • Working proficiency in Hebrew reading and writing

To Apply

Please submit a cover letter and resume or CV to jobs@sefaria.org with the subject line Part-time writer/editor. Applications will be accepted on a rolling basis until the position is filled.
