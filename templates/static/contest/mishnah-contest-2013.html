Can you translate Hebrew?

Help Sefaria create the first freely reusable Mishnah translation - and win a free iPad!
This contest has ended! Read about the winners on our blog.
When: December 15th - December 31st
Where: www.sefaria.org/mishnah-contest-2013
What: A new iPad Air for the top contributor. A new iPad Mini each for the first and second runners up.
How: Visit the contest page, get your assignment, and enter your original translation. Follow your progress on our leaderboard; spread the word to everyone you know, and see who can contribute the most translations!
Why: You will be making some of the core texts of the Jewish tradition accessible in English to anyone who is interested in studying them! Plus, you could win an iPad.

The Details:

  • Translations must be your own original translations, and must follow our translation guidelines.
  • Points are awarded based on the length of the translated texts (the longer the Mishnah, the more points you earn).
  • The Sefaria team will review the work of the top contributors and reserves the right to disqualify translations that we believe do not adequately conform to our translation guidelines.
  • Prizes will be awarded to the top three contributors based on points.
