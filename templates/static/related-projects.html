Related Projects

  • Wikisource: Hebrew / English - The best collection of publicly licensed Jewish texts on the internet. The bulk of texts which have gotten Sefaria off the ground were resued from Wikisource.
  • The Open Siddur Project - Pioneers of Open Source Jewish tech, creating free texts and technology around Jewish liturgy.
  • On1Foot - A database of Jewish texts related to to social justice, as well as a source sheet builder.
  • Hebrewbooks.org - Scanned images of over 50,000 classical Jewish books.
  • Mercava - Big visions for Jewish tech, including a beautiful recreation of the Daf in HTML.
  • TaggedTanakh - Open annotations for Tanakh.
  • Psookim - Multimedia presentations of Jewish texts.
  • Mechon Mamre - A great, free collection of texts online, but under copyright.
  • The Responsa Project - The biggest collection of digitized Jewish texts, available for a price.
  • JHacker.org - Great blog and hub for Judaism and technology.
