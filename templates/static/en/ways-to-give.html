Donation FAQs

How will I receive my donation receipt?

If you donate online, you will receive an e-receipt. If you donate by check or wire, you will receive a receipt by mail.

Can I donate in shekels and/or receive an Israeli tax receipt?

Unfortunately, we are not set up to receive donations in shekels or issue Israeli tax receipts as this point. We hope to be able to offer these options to Sefaria supporters in Israel in the future. If you have questions about making a gift from Israel, please contact donate@sefaria.org.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Donations to Sefaria are tax deductible as allowed by law. We are a registered charity in the United States (Sefaria's EIN is 46-4406454). Currently, we cannot issue tax receipts in Israel, the United Kingdom, or Canada.

Can I donate by eCheck?

Yes, you can donate by eCheck. Please enter the mailing address listed above.
